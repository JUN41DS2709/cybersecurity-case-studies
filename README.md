# Cybersecurity Case Studies

A curated collection of documented cybersecurity incidents, data breaches, major vulnerabilities, and global cyber attacks analyzed from both offensive and defensive perspectives.

This repository serves as a knowledge base for studying how real-world attacks happened, why they succeeded, their impact, and the lessons they left behind.

The goal is not to reproduce exploits or provide attack walkthroughs, but to understand the technical, operational, and strategic aspects of cybersecurity incidents across industries and threat landscapes.

## Coverage

This repository documents:

* Major data breaches
* Global cyber attacks and campaigns
* Critical and high-impact CVEs
* Ransomware incidents
* Supply chain compromises
* Nation-state and APT operations
* Cloud and infrastructure breaches
* Misconfiguration-related incidents
* Insider threats
* Emerging threats and recent attacks

## Analysis Framework

Each case study may include:

* Executive Summary
* Timeline of Events
* Threat Actor Profile
* Attack Vector
* Vulnerabilities Exploited
* Initial Access Method
* Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs)
* Impact Assessment
* Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)
* Detection Opportunities
* Mitigation and Remediation
* Lessons Learned
* References and Further Reading
